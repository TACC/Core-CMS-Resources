The Frontera software stack is the ‘secret sauce’ to its success. The base is the Linux distribution Centos 7. On top of that, throw in some 200-plus custom-built packages based on the RPM format. Add two base compilers, finely chopped. Next, two whole base MPI stacks. Top it off with a pinch of 40 software libraries and many community MPI-enabled applications for zest. Spicy!
